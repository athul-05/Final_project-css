_About Me_


Hello everyone, I'm Cristiano Ronaldo, and I want to share a little bit about myself. I was born on February 5, 1985, in Madeira, Portugal. From a very young age, I knew football was my passion, and I put everything I had into making it my career. Growing up, I faced challenges, but I always believed in myself and kept pushing to be the best I could be.

Throughout my career, I've had the privilege of playing for some of the biggest clubs in the world—Sporting CP, Manchester United, Real Madrid, Juventus, and now Al Nassr. Each chapter has been an incredible journey, filled with hard work, dedication, and unforgettable moments. I've been fortunate enough to win many titles, including multiple Ballon d'Ors and Champions League trophies, but the real reward for me has always been the love and support of my fans and the opportunity to inspire others to follow their dreams.

When I'm on the pitch, my goal is simple: to give everything I have to my team and my fans. Whether I'm scoring goals, creating opportunities, or just pushing myself to the limit, I always strive for greatness. But beyond the game, I'm also committed to making a difference in the world. Through my foundation, I try to give back to those in need and help those who are less fortunate.

Football has given me so much, but it's the lessons I've learned through the game—about perseverance, hard work, and resilience—that shape who I am. I am constantly looking to improve, whether it's on the field or as a person. Thank you to everyone who has supported me along the way—this journey is as much yours as it is mine.
